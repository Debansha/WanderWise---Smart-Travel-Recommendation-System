🛡️ General Safety

  • Keep copies of documents

    Store digital copies in cloud storage and carry physical photocopies separately from originals

  • Share your itinerary

    Always share your travel plans with a trusted family member or friend

  • Get travel insurance

    Never travel without comprehensive travel and medical insurance

  • Register with your embassy

    Register your trip with your country's embassy at your destination

🏥 Health & Wellness

  • Check vaccination requirements

    Some countries require specific vaccinations — check 6-8 weeks before travel

  • Stay hydrated

    Carry a reusable water bottle. In some countries, only drink bottled or filtered water

  • Pack a first-aid kit

    Include band-aids, antiseptic, pain relievers, anti-diarrhea medicine, and any prescription meds

  • Sun protection

    Apply SPF 50+ sunscreen every 2 hours, wear hats, and stay in shade during peak hours

💳 Money Safety

  • Use money belts

    Keep valuables hidden in a money belt under your clothes

  • Notify your bank

    Tell your bank about your travel plans to avoid card blocks

  • Carry multiple payment methods

    Cash, debit card, credit card — never rely on just one

🎒 On-the-Go Safety

  • Keep bags close

    Use anti-theft backpacks and keep your bag in front in crowded areas

  • Be aware of scams

    Research common tourist scams at your destination beforehand

  • Save offline maps

    Download Google Maps offline before your trip in case of no internet

Incognito Flights

Search flights in incognito mode — airlines raise prices when they see repeated searches!

📧

Price Alerts

Set up Google Flights price alerts to get notified when fares drop.

🧊

Freeze Water Bottles

Freeze half-full water bottles the night before — cold water all day long!

📸

Photo of Hotel

Take a photo of your hotel's business card or address — show it to taxi drivers!

👟

Shower Cap Shoes

Put dirty shoes in shower caps to keep your suitcase clean.

🔌

Binder Clip Charger

Use binder clips to organize charging cables and prevent them from tangling.

🗺️

Free Walking Tours

Most cities offer free walking tours — tip-based, so pay what you think it's worth!

🏨

Late Check-out

Always ask for late check-out — it's free 70% of the time!

⚖️

Roll, Don't Fold

Rolling clothes saves 30% more space than folding and prevents wrinkles!
