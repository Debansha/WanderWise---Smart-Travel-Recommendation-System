Money Saving Tips

🎫 Book flights 6-8 weeks in advance ▼

Studies show that the sweet spot for domestic flights is 1-3 months before departure, and for international flights, 2-8 months ahead.

🏨 Use hotel comparison sites ▼

Compare prices across Booking.com, Agoda, and Hostelworld. Also consider Airbnb for longer stays — weekly discounts can save 20-30%.

🍜 Eat where locals eat ▼

Avoid tourist-area restaurants. Street food and local markets offer authentic cuisine at a fraction of the price.

🚌 Use public transport ▼

Taxis and ride-shares add up fast. Research city passes and metro cards — many cities offer tourist passes that bundle transport with attractions.

📅 Travel during shoulder season ▼

Shoulder season (just before or after peak) offers great weather, fewer crowds, and prices 20-40% lower than peak season.
