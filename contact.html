Frequently Asked Questions

Is WanderWise free to use? ▼

Yes! WanderWise is completely free. We offer premium features for power users, but all core features including trip planning, budget calculator, and weather dashboard are free forever.

How accurate are the recommendations? ▼

Our AI-powered recommendation engine analyzes your preferences against thousands of data points. The more details you provide, the better your matches! Most users find 90%+ accuracy.

Can I save and share my itineraries? ▼

Absolutely! You can save itineraries to your browser, export them as text files, and share them via email or social media. Premium users get cloud sync across devices.

Do you book flights and hotels? ▼

Currently, we focus on planning and recommendations. We partner with booking platforms and provide direct links to the best deals. Integrated booking is coming soon!

Is the weather data real-time? ▼

Our weather dashboard uses historical averages and seasonal patterns to give you reliable planning data. For real-time conditions, we recommend checking closer to your travel date.
