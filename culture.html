🏮 Gion Matsuri

🎆 Obon Festival

August — Festival honoring ancestors with dances and lanterns

Do's & Don'ts

✅ Do's

  • Bow when greeting people
  • Remove shoes before entering homes
  • Say "Itadakimasu" before eating
  • Carry cash — many places don't accept cards

❌ Don'ts

  • Don't tip — it can be considered rude
  • Don't eat while walking on the street
  • Don't talk loudly on public transport
  • Don't stick chopsticks upright in rice

Do's & Don'ts

✅ Do's

  • Remove shoes at temples and homes
  • Use right hand for eating and greetings
  • Dress modestly at religious sites
  • Try street food — it's amazing!

❌ Don'ts

  • Don't point feet at people or deities
  • Don't display public affection
  • Don't touch someone's head
  • Don't drink tap water
